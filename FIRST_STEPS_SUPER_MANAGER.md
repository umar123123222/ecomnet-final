## Pro Tips for Success

### Inventory Optimization
- **Review daily:** Low stock alerts and smart reorder recommendations
- **Redistribute proactively:** Don't wait for stock-outs
- **Use data:** Check sales velocity before approving transfers
- **Communicate:** Coordinate with outlet managers on upcoming promotions

### Purchase Order Efficiency
- **Batch orders:** Group products by supplier to reduce PO count
- **Use lead times:** Order in advance to avoid rush fees
- **Build relationships:** Maintain good supplier relationships for flexibility
- **Monitor performance:** Switch suppliers if consistently poor quality/delays

### Fraud Prevention
- **Trust the system:** Fraud detection is trained on patterns
- **Investigate high-risk:** Always contact customer before approving high-risk orders
- **Document decisions:** Add notes explaining why you approved/rejected
- **Watch for patterns:** Serial returners and address mismatches

### Returns Management
- **Be fair but firm:** Follow return policy consistently
- **Inspect thoroughly:** Take photos before restocking
- **Track patterns:** High return rates indicate product/process issues
- **Fast refunds:** Process refunds quickly for legitimate returns
